Vegans deserve better than a fruit salad
by Ardita Hardi

Even though veganism is booming, it seems that most caterers and chefs still feel that desserts can only be created with cow secretions and chicken eggs… Weird. After 25 years as a vegan, I have gotten used to going without dessert in a restaurant or simply enjoying something at home — when I first became vegan, I was just happy with a bit of dairyfree chocolate or a cheeky apple pie with Swedish Glace! But we deserve much more than that. Of course, things are getting better — with some nice vegan trifles and chocolate mousses popping up in supermarkets, and even some interesting delights in the chain restaurants.

Veganic gardening tips: Dealing with pests
by Ardita Hardi

Occasionally you may find you need to supplement your other protection methods by relocating a few repeat offenders! This is especially relevant for slugs and snails. In this case, relocating means gathering them up in a bucket or lidded container and taking them at least 100 metres away; snails are homing creatures so lobbing them over the garden fence certainly won’t work!
